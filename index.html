"Beauty is a by-product of Love being poured out"

I see a Generation of Artists waking up. Young creatives who have left behind the ideals of societal criticism and political motivation. Artists who pour all their passion, sorrow and love into their creations. They are not trying to move mountains. They move hearts. What they create is astoundingly beautiful.
